About Us


We're an indie game studio that's made of a couple people at the moment , a programmer/developer , a 3D artist , and a music composer.

We try to focus on "worth-doing" games to develop , we try to find a certain problem and find a solution for it by developing a game!


Since August 2016 , We started working on an environmental survival game called "Territory". It is the first game to be developed by us under the name "Low Eyed Studios" , we had a name before called "Indie Master Productions" and released many games which were developed for learning purposes. We took this project very seriously and now it's running a crowdfunding campaign on PixelFunder.com!
